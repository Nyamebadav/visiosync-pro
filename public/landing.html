VisioSync Pro

One idea → full music video

Type your concept, pick a genre and mood. Claude AI drafts the lyrics, writes the scene blueprint, and generates your visual storyboard. xAI renders the images and video clips. You export the final cut.

  • 🎵
    One-Idea Creator — Claude writes complete lyrics and a scene-by-scene blueprint from a single prompt.
  • 🖼️
    Image Studio — xAI generates cinematic still frames for every scene in your storyboard.
  • 🎬
    Video Clips — xAI renders 5-second motion clips; FFmpeg stitches them with your audio track.
  • 💰
    Session Cost Tracker — Live USD counter for every Claude token and xAI generation.

About Medase Unitelle

Built by a creator, for creators

Medase Unitelle ("thank you universe" in Twi) is an independent AI studio building creative tools that give independent musicians and filmmakers the power of a full production house.

🌍

African creative voice

Genre lists, moods, and AI prompts are tuned for Afrobeats, Highlife, Gospel, and Afropop — not just Western pop.

⚡

Speed over friction

No complex timelines by default. One idea in, full storyboard out. Complexity is opt-in.

🔐

Transparent & honest

API keys stay server-side. Every AI call's real cost shows in the live cost tracker — no surprise bills.
